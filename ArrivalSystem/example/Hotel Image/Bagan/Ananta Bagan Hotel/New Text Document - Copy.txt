With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

In addition, all guestrooms feature a variety of comforts. Many rooms even provide television LCD/plasma screen, internet access – wireless (complimentary), non smoking rooms, air conditioning, wake-up service to please the most discerning guest. The hotel offers many unique recreational opportunities such as outdoor pool, spa, massage, garden. Whatever your purpose of visit, Ananta Bagan Hotel is an excellent choice for your stay in Bagan.
